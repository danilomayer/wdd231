Site Plan - Alana Macedo RH Consultoria

Site Name

Name: Alana Macedo Consultoria de RH

Reason: This site is intended to hold information and services for my wife's business of HR Consultancy.

Optional Domain Availability: alanamacedoconsultoria.com.br

Site Purpose

The intention of building this site is to provide a place for the client to showcase their business qualities and background, offer to potential customers an opportunity to solve their problems and allow them to apply for a specialized b2b service.

Scenarios

  • A small business owner is looking for HR consultancy services to improve their hiring process. They visit the site to learn about the services offered and contact the consultant for a personalized solution.
  • An HR manager is searching for a consultant to provide training sessions for their team. They explore the site to review the consultant's expertise and schedule a meeting.

Color Schema

  • Primary Color: #FFE5D9 - Used for headings and accents.
  • Secondary Color: #725e64 - Used for backgrounds and paragraphs.

Typography

  • Font 1: "Courgette", cursive - Used for headings.
  • Font 2: "Poiret One", sans-serif - Used for body text.
