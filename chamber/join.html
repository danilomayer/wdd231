Benefits

Non-Profit Membership:

Support our community and contribute to local growth as a non-profit member.

  • Recognition as a community supporter.
  • Access to networking events.
  • Inclusion in community outreach programs.

Bronze Membership:

The Bronze membership is designed for businesses looking to establish a strong presence in the community.

  • Basic listing in the chamber directory.
  • Invitations to business seminars and workshops.
  • Access to member-exclusive newsletters.

Silver Membership:

Elevate your business with the Silver membership, offering enhanced visibility and opportunities.

  • Upgraded listing with a logo in the chamber directory.
  • Priority access to networking events.
  • Featured spot in the chamber's promotional materials.
  • Discounts on chamber-sponsored programs.

Gold Membership:

Maximize your impact and influence with the Gold membership, tailored for businesses seeking premium benefits.

  • Prominent logo placement in all chamber communications.
  • VIP access to exclusive events and business leader forums.
  • Dedicated promotional opportunities through the chamber's media channels.
  • Priority sponsorship opportunities for chamber events.
  • Access to advanced business development programs.
